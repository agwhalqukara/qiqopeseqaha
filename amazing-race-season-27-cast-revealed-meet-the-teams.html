    The world is waiting for them! Eleven new teams of Amazing Race fans have officially kicked off their journey across the globe in a quest to nab the show's $1 million prize. Starting the race in Venice, Calif., on Monday, June 22, 11 teams of two — from a mother and son, to BFFs, and even members of the paparazzi — started their trek with support from series host Phil Keoghan.

    Upon completing their first challenge at the Venice boardwalk's Graffiti Wall, the teams raced to Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and will continue racing until one team is named the winner of The Amazing Race. They'll take over the title from 2015 winners Laura Pierson and Tyler Adams, who competed as a blind date couple last season.

    Can't wait for this fall's TAR premiere? Meet the teams now!
