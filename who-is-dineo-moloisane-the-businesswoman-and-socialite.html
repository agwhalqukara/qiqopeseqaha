    Dineo Moloisane was Born in the Early 90s

    Dineo Moloisane was born in a city in Pretoria called Atteridgeville in the early 1990s. However, the exact year, month, and date her parents gave to her are not known to the public. The radio presenter hasn’t been forthcoming about her age, as she doesn’t deliberately talk about it.

    The socialite was raised in a supportive family. However, the names of other members of her family are not public knowledge. She attended Tsogo Secondary School, an English-medium high school situated on the grounds of the Most Holy Redeemer Catholic Church in Mmakau, northwest of the City of Tshwane in the North West Province of South Africa.

    Dineo attended the Tshwane University of Technology for her tertiary education. Her course of study and graduation date is not available for public consumption.

    She is a Respected Radio Presenter 

    Dineo Moloisane currently works with the Pretoria-based community radio, Pheli FM, as a presenter. Not much is publicly known about her career with the station. However, she began her career in the media industry after she graduated from college.

    The former rapper is a household name across major social media platforms. She is most influential on Instagram, where over 300,000 people are currently following her. Dineo is most active on Instagram and regularly posts photos of her products and day-to-day activities on the platform.

    Her Twitter account has about 5,180 followers, but she is hardly seen on the platform. The last time she posted on Twitter was in 2021. Moloisane is followed by over 4,000 people on Facebook. She basically shares official posts about her work and gigs on the platform.

    Dineo Malaisane Owns Multiple Local Business Ventures

    The radio presenter is the CEO of Magic Diet and Magic Skin beauty line. She founded the Magic Diet brand in 2018 after the birth of her first child, while her Magic Skin line followed two years later. The socialite is also the founder of the Traveling with King Dineo enterprise.

    She is the CEO of the makeup brand Brown Fleek with King Dineo. Beyond her looks, this beautiful South African celebrity is really a hard-working woman.

    As a rapper, she released a song titled PRAY (featuring Emtee Reason Mosankie & Tooly B) in 2020 before abandoning the art shortly after.
