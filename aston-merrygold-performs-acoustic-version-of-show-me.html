    Aston Merrygold performs acoustic version of 'Show Me'

    Former JLS hearthrob Aston Merrygold has performed an acoustic version of his latest hit single Show Me. Aston performed the acoustic version of his new track at the I Heart Radio Live, three days ago.

    26 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Former JLS hearthrob Aston Merrygold has performed an acoustic version of his latest hit single ‘Show Me’.

    Aston performed the acoustic version of his new track at the I Heart Radio Live, three days ago.

    Singing the track acoustically, allows the 27 year old’s vocal to shine.

    The new single ‘Show Me’ is taken from Merrygold’s upcoming debut album ‘Show Stopper’.

    The new LP was due for release in October of this year, after the release of his debut top 40 UK hit  single ‘Get Stupid’, but the release date has now been pushed back and a UK date is currently unknown at the moment.

    ‘Show Stopper’ is being released in Australia before anywhere else in the world, as is his new single ‘Show Me’.

    Aston also performed ‘Show Me’ on the Australian version of The X Factor earlier this month.

    UK fans of the singer may have a while to wait for the album to drop here, but I’m sure it will be well worth the wait.

    With songs like ‘Get Stupid’ and ‘Show Me’, the former boyband singer, is well on his way to becoming a huge solo star.
