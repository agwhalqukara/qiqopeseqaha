    It’s been a long time since Sean Lowe was considered a Bachelor. In fact, the former reality star is coming up on his one-year wedding anniversary to wife Catherine Giudici on Jan. 26, an impressive feat considering the amount of Bachelor Nation couples who have actually made it to the altar intact. So on Jan. 19, Lowe, 31, chose to reflect on his year of marriage in a new blog post titled, “10 Things I’ve Learned Since My Wedding Day.”

    Some of his revelations (“Life is better when you can share it with someone else” and “Waking up next to my best friend every day is the best!”) were seemingly trite, while others (“Bravo will be played in my house and I have to deal with it”) were more personalized and funny. 

    Point No. 2, “I’m not always right,” was a lesson many married men could relate to. 

    “Not only am I not always right, but I find now that I’m married I’m usually wrong. Other husbands have told me this is normal,” Lowe wrote.
