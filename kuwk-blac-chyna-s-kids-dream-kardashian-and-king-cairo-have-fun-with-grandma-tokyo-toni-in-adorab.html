    Tokyo Toni took to social media to post a cute clip of her grandkids, Dream and King Cairo having some sweet sibling fun. Furthermore, Blac Chyna’s mother couldn’t help but gush over how smart the little girl is.

    The footage shows the 2 year old having the time of her life while playing with her older half-brother, 6, and their grandma.

    Tokyo Toni was the one to share the adorable family video with the world, proving that she loves nothing more than to just hang out with the young ones.

    It shows mainly Dream but also King as he paints with Toni and his younger sister.

    The 2 year old looks precious in an apron and also has a pink bow in her hair as she gives her grandma the paint colors needed for the painting session.

    They can be seen sitting in front of a white canvas, ready to let their artistic skills shine.

    At the same time, the 6 year old brother seems just as interested in the activity, painting and talking to his grandmother about what else they need for a great picture.
