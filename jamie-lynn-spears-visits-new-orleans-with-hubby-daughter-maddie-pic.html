    Family pic! Jamie Lynn Spears spent a beautiful day in New Orleans, Louisiana on Saturday, Nov. 8 with her husband Jamie Watson and daughter Maddie, 6. The proud mom, 23, posted a photo in the town's famed Jackson Square, standing in front of the statue of Andrew Jackson and the Saint Louis Cathedral. 

    "#nofilter #nola," she captioned the photo. 

    Spears tied the knot with Watson in the culturally-rich city back in June in a "very emotional" ceremony. Little Maddie served as flower girl and her cousins Sean and Jayden (the sons of Jamie Lynn's sister Britney Spears) wore black tuxedos with white bow ties. The Spears family hails from Kentwood, Louisiana.
