    It's another girl for Eric Benet! The singer and his wife, Manuela Testolini, welcomed their second child on Sunday, July 13, his rep confirms exclusively to Us Weekly.

    The couple's daughter, Amoura Luna, arrived (with a full head of hair!) at 6 lbs. and 13 oz.

    The little tot has an adorable dimple on her right cheek and was given the middle name "Luna" because she was born on the night of a supermoon, the rep tells Us.

    Us broke news in March that Benet, 47, and Testolini, 37, were expecting their second child together. The couple welcomed daughter Lucia Bella in December 2011.
