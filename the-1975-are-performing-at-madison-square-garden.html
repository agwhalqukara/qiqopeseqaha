    MUST. STAY. CALM. MUST. KEEP. BREATHING…Okay, it isn’t working. We don’t know about you guys but, as massive fans of The 1975, we are totally losing our shizzle right now.

    The group are set to perform at MSG. Yes, you heard us, MADISON SQUARE GARDEN, for the first time, and we refuse to apologise for our emotional state.

    The 1975, a band consisting of Matthew Healy, Adam Hann, George Daniel and Ross MacDonald, released their second studio album, titled ‘I Like It When You Sleep For You Are So Beautiful Yet So Unaware Of It’ last year and, since then, they seem to have just gone from strength to strength. The group started out only playing to small crowds, putting on really intimate, immersive shows for their fans, however, since their fanbase has grown in size in a way which could not have been anticipated, the venues they’re playing have grown in size too.
