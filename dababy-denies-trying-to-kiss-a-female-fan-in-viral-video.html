    09 Jul 2024, 00:00

    A video that led fans to believe DaBaby attempted to kiss a female fan went viral on the internet, but the rapper has denied it. DaBaby was in hot water a year ago for remarks he made during his Rolling Loud speech. Now, the rapper has explained the viral video in which he was seen attempting to kiss a fan. He also shared an extended version of the viral video.

    Dababy Tried To Kiss A Fan

    On Thursday, April 7, a video of DaBaby having a moment with his fans went viral on the Internet. In a clip from the full video, the rapper pulls a female fan close to him while cupping her face for a kiss. The girl, on the other hand, appears to reject him by turning away. People soon began to share their thoughts on the video based on what they saw. The video was shared on a variety of popular social media platforms, including DJ Academics.

    The overlay text in DJ Akademics’ viral video reads: “Da Baby tried to kiss his fan and she curved him lmao.”

    Rapper Clears The Air

    DaBaby took to Instagram to deny all allegations of forcing himself on a fan. The rapper reshared the viral post on his Instagram story, writing, “Ya’ll Go head-on man. “My booboos and I love each other to death, but there’s no kissin going on.”

    He also commented on The Shade Room’s post, referencing the fan who refused his kiss. “I’m sorry, bae,” the comment said.

    “Bae was upset that I blew a kiss at her lil buddy first,” another comment read. TSR edited the original post to detail what happened after the rapper provided an explanation.

    He Is No Stranger To Controversy

    DaBaby was recently in the news for asking women to undress during a pre-Grammys performance, according to Variety.

    The rapper was performing on stage at Las Vegas’ Drai’s Beach Club. “I don’t discriminate,” he allegedly said before telling female fans to “pull your t*****s out if you love DaBaby.”
