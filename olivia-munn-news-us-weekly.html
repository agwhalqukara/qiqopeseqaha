    Name:Olivia Munn

    Hometown:Oklahoma City, Oklahoma

    Birthday:July 3, 1980

    About

    Olivia Munn began her television career as a cohost on G4 network’s Attack of the Show! from 2006 to 2010. She then stepped into a correspondent role on The Daily Show from 2010 to 2011. Munn eventually landed a lead role on HBO’s The Newsroom, which ran from 2012 to 2015 before starring as Psylocke in 2016’s X-Men: Apocalypse.

    Munn dated Aaron Rodgers from 2014 to 2017 before moving on with John Mulaney, with whom she shares son Malcolm, born in 2021.
